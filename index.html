Background

During my engineering training at EPITA with a specialization in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, I have gained valuable Machibe Learning experience through internships at Hexaglobe, focusing on anomaly detection on time-series, and at Airbus Defence and Space, working on semantic segmentation of very high-resolution satellite images (Pléiades).

Currently, I hold the position of AI Research Engineer at IRT Saint Exupery, where I am actively involved in two exciting projects. In the first one, named RAPTOR, my primary focus is to develop an autonomous pose estimation system for spacecraft rendezvous with neural networks. Additionally, I am engaged in the Confiance.ai project (Grand Défi program on "Securing, certifying, and enhancing the reliability of systems based on artificial intelligence.") to determine the optimal implementation of various Machine Learning models on multiple embedded platforms, with a particular focus on FPGAs.

With a strong affinity for space applications, my research interests focus on the development of efficient Machine Learning models and their deployment on embedded systems.
